About The Secret Crime Agency

Uncovering the secrets that protect our world. We work in the shadows, ensuring safety at every turn.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to protect our nation and its people from threats, both foreign and domestic. With cutting-edge intelligence, covert operations, and highly skilled agents, we maintain peace in the most dangerous corners of the world.

Global Security, Local Focus

The SCA works on multiple fronts — from cyber threats to human trafficking and terrorism. We stop the most dangerous individuals before they can act. Our agents operate silently and swiftly.

Our History

Founded in 1955, the SCA began as a small intelligence unit, growing into a global force for peace. For over 70 years, we’ve adapted to meet the ever-evolving threats that challenge world security.

Decades of Excellence

From our first covert mission to today's advanced operations, we’ve built a legacy of courage, resilience, and success. The SCA has earned global recognition for its unmatched ability to neutralize high-stakes threats.

Our Operatives

Our operatives are the heart and soul of the agency. They are highly trained professionals who carry out the most complex missions with precision and secrecy. They are selected for their skills, resilience, and ability to handle high-pressure situations.

Agents of Change

Each agent undergoes rigorous training, mastering various fields — from cyber intelligence to hand-to-hand combat. Their discipline and dedication are unmatched, ensuring that no mission is impossible.

Global Reach

Our operations span continents, ensuring that we are always a step ahead of global threats. With agents embedded in key locations worldwide, we ensure stability and security across the globe.

On Every Continent

Whether in a bustling city or the most remote areas, our agents are always ready to face the toughest challenges. Our global presence allows us to respond swiftly to emerging threats, keeping our world safe.
